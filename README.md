# SupaMates AI — Supermacs Loyalty Intelligence System

> *Ireland's most beloved fast food brand has 320,000 weekly customers and zero loyalty profiles. SupaMates AI exists to change that.*

A customer intelligence and loyalty platform concept built for Supermacs — Ireland's largest homegrown fast food chain — as part of the MSc Artificial Intelligence for Business programme at National College of Ireland (2026).

---

## The Problem

| | Supermacs | McDonald's |
|---|---|---|
| Weekly customers | 320,000 | — |
| Irish locations | 118+ | — |
| Annual revenue | €314M | — |
| Active loyalty profiles | **0** | **185,000,000** |

Supermacs won a landmark trademark battle against McDonald's in the EU courts. But in the loyalty data war, the gap is enormous. Every customer walks in anonymous and leaves a stranger — no personalisation, no retention data, no loyalty engine.

---

## The Solution

SupaMates AI is a loyalty intelligence system that segments customers, personalises their experience, and builds the data layer Supermacs has never had.

### Features

**Customer Segmentation Quiz**
An interactive quiz that classifies customers into loyalty personas based on ordering behaviour and visit patterns — e.g. Snack Box Loyalist, Late Night Regular, Family Visitor.

**Loyalty Dashboard**
A mobile-first dashboard showing:
- Points balance and progress to next tier
- Loyalty tier: Mate → SupaFan → Gaffer
- Personalised offers based on customer type
- Visit streaks and reward history

**Tier System**
Three-tier loyalty programme with unlockable rewards and streak bonuses, designed around Supermacs' actual product range and customer segments.

**AI Personalisation Layer**
Offer recommendations driven by customer type classification — the quiz feeds into a segmentation model that determines which offers, messages, and rewards a customer sees.
